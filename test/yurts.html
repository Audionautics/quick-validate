What is a yurt?
Out luxury yurts are permanant structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Eacch yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. the luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot water and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
